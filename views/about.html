About MovieLibrary

MovieLibrary is a modern platform designed for movie enthusiasts who want to organize, track, and manage their personal film collections.

Whether you are building a watchlist, archiving your favorite classics, or managing a growing collection, MovieLibrary helps you keep everything in one place.

What you can do

  • Browse and explore movies
  • Add and edit movie information
  • Organize movies by release year
  • Securely manage your collection with authentication

MovieLibrary is continuously evolving to provide a smooth and secure experience for all users.
