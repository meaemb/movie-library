About MovieLibrary

MovieLibrary is a modern platform for people who love movies and want to organize their personal collections in a clean and simple way.

Whether you are building a watchlist, tracking watched movies, or managing a growing archive, MovieLibrary keeps everything in one place.

What you can do

  • Browse and explore your movie collection
  • Add, edit, and delete movies
  • Filter movies by release year
  • Protect your collection with login access
